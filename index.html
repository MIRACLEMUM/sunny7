Why do people love to eat cakes ?

Whenever you are feeling down or low, just eat a piece of cake, and it will lift your mood. The primary ingredient in chocolate cake is cocoa, which helps brain release the “feel good” hormone. Therefore, when you eat cake, it lightens up your mood and makes you feel much better.
